Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Necromancer's Rise respects your privacy. The game itself does not collect or store personal information on our own servers — your game progress is saved locally on your device.

The app uses the following third-party services, which may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — may use device identifiers to serve ads. For users in the EEA/UK, ad personalization is governed by the consent choice shown in-app. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) — processes purchases through your Google account. We never see or store your payment details.

The game does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of consent. You can reset your advertising preferences at any time from your device settings.
